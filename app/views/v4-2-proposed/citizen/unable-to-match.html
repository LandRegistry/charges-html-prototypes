Unable to match you to the mortgage transaction

We regret we have been unable to match your identity to a current mortgage transaction.

There are a few reasons why this might happen, for example if you’ve recently had a change of circumstances.

What happens next?

Your lender and conveyancer have been notified that you have not been able to digitally sign your mortgage deed.

The person dealing with your mortgage will be in touch in the coming days to update you on what to do next.

GDS Feedback

There needs to be an adequate route that provides a proper path forward, with content explaining what to do next.
