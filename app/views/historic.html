Version 3 ... to 03 Dec 2015

Version 3 is primarily about maximising the borrowers understanding of what they are doing/agreeing to. Make what the borrower needs to agree to ('sign') to get their money, understandable, and show the status of the thing (who has agreed/yet to agree/what happens next). Break the deed down into the legal/logical parts/steps - and explain legal 'clauses' in 'plain English'. Show the full thing at the end for signing - in a format that relates to the previous steps.
