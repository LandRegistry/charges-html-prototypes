1. This mortgage incorporates the Mortgage Conditions and Explanations, a copy of which has been received by the Borrower, and the rules (for the time being) of the Society.

  2. The Borrower charges (by way of legal mortgage and with full title guarantee) the property with payment of all monies payable by the Borrower to the Society.

  3. The Society has power under the mortgage conditions to make further advances secured by the mortgage.

  4. The Borrower and the Society shall jointly apply to the Chief Land Registrar to enter the following restriction in the Proprietorship Register of the title number referred to:

    “No disposition of the registered estate by the proprietor of the registered estate or by the proprietor of any registered charge, is to be registered without a written consent signed by the proprietor for the time being of the charge dated ... ... ... in favour of the Society referred to in the Charge Register.”
