Step 6 of 7 Obligation

Bank of England PLC has power under the mortgage conditions to make further advances secured by the mortgage and is obliged to do so.

This means that the lender is required to lend you further sums of money in the future.

Example:

If a property needed major repairs, the lender and the borrower could agree that money will be borrowed in instalments as the work is completed.
