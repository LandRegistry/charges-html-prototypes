Read your mortgage: Step 5 of 7 Restriction

The lender and the borrower apply to the Chief Land Registrar for the following restriction to be entered on the register: “No disposition of the registered estate by the proprietor of the registered estate is to be registered without a written consent signed by the proprietor for the time being of the Charge date ___________ in favour of Bank of England Ltd referred to in the Charges Register”.

This means that while you owe money to the lender they’ll need to agree before you can grant a lease or take out an additional mortgage on the property.
