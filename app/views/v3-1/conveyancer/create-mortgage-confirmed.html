What happens next

Your borrower(s) now need to visit the GOV.UK website to sign their mortgage deed. When all borrowers have signed, you will be able to apply to register.

The link and some suggested explanatory text have been provided below:

To sign your mortgage deed, please follow the attached link. You will be asked to identify yourself using GOV.UK Verify, and you can then read and sign your mortgage deed. Everyone on the mortgage deed will need to complete this process to submit the application.

You will need:

  • your passport and/or driving licence
  • a bank/credit card statement, if possible
  • the phone number and/or email address you've given us
