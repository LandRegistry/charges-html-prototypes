Charging clause

The Mortgagor with full title guarantee charges the Property by way of legal mortgage with payment of all moneys, obligation and liabilities whatsoever whether for principal interest or otherwise which may now or at any time in the future be due owing or incurred by the Mortgagor and/or Borrower to the Lender (the “Secured Liabilities” as defined in the Conditions).

Restriction

If the property is, or includes, registered land, the Lender and the Mortgagor(s) apply to the Chief Land Registrar for a restriction to be entered on the register that, no disposition of the registered estate by the proprietor of the registered estate is to be registered without a written consent signed by the proprietor for the time being of the Charge date in favour of Bank of England Ltd referred to in the Charges Register.

The Society has power under the mortgage conditions to make further advances secured by the mortgage and is obliged to do so.
