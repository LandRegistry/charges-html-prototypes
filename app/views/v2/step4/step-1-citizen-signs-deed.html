Sign your mortgage deed

Use this service to confirm your mortgage details are correct, and sign your mortgage deed. Your conveyancer wil then ensure the Land Registry is updated.

To do this you will need:

  • To verify your identity on GOV.UK Verify
  • The phone number and/or email address you provided to your lender

GOV.UK Verify will pass your information to the Land Registry for the purposes of fraud prevention and detection. This helps protect your interests. Are you happy for the Land Registry to hold this information indefinitely?
