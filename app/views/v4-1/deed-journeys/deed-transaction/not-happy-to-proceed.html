If you are not happy to proceed

If there's a mistake in your mortgage deed, or you are not happy to agree to the terms, inform your conveyancer as soon as possible.

The best way to do this is to get back in touch with the person who asked you to sign the deed.

If you have selected “No” in error, just go back to continue signing your mortgage deed.
