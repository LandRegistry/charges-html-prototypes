Details confirmed

Now you've confirmed your details, you can view and digitally sign your mortgage deed. The steps below explain what you'll need to do next.

  1. Check your details

    Check your personal details and the property address on the deed to confirm that they are correct

  2. Check your lender and read their clauses

    Check the lender you instructed is displayed on the deed and read through their charging clauses and provisions

  3. Sign the deed

    Use the button at the bottom of the deed to acknowledge that you have read the information on the deed and wish to sign it
